Website Bio

This is a website made to show information about who Basheer is. Born in Detroit, MI in December of 2005 he is 16 years old and will be 18 years old in late 2023.

He was raised in many different areas around on Detroit's Westside. Growing up he was also part of the Hidden Genius Project

The hidden genius project trains and mentors black male youth in technology creation, entrepreneurship, and leadership skills to transform their lives and communities.
Detroit is a city that at the time had been known for being one of the most dangerous cities in the United States. With 75% of Detroit Residents being African American, over 30% of the population living in poverty, along with a high unemployment rate of 5% compared to the average unemployment rate of 3.6% in the U. S.
The hidden genius project started in California. In 2022 the hidden genius project added another location based in Detroit, MI. Which was a city with mostly African people and a city that was struggling to evolve. That's part of what made the hidden genius project expand to Detroit. To help teenagers to grow as a person, learn skills they need, and gain support to go in a better direction. The members of the Hidden Genius Project are also known for being well-rounded and educated people. This is why being a part of the program was a major part of learning and growing for Basheer at such a young age.
